## LANTERN-RD-RAW  
*Comprehensive Dataset of Invasive Spotted Lanternfly Imagery*  
[GitHub Repository](https://github.com/srkvatsa/LANTERN-RD-RAW)

Developed LANTERN-RD, an extensive dataset focusing on the invasive spotted lanternfly (SLF). This work was presented at CVPR and NeurIPS workshops and has been inducted into the Harvard-NASA ADS. The dataset aims to aid in the mitigation of SLF through deep learning applications.

## INFO 1998: Introduction to Machine Learning  
*Lecturer for Cornell University's INFO 1998 Course*  
[Course Website](https://cornelldatascience.github.io/info1998/)
